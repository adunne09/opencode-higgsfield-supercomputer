## What I do

I assist with songwriting craft (structure, rhyme, meter, hooks), generate prompts optimized for AI music tools like Suno, and apply parody and lyric adaptation techniques including phonetic tricks.

## When to use me

Use me when writing song lyrics, creating AI music prompts, adapting existing songs, or learning songwriting techniques. Trigger phrases: "write a song", "create lyrics", "make a Suno prompt", "parody this song".

## Inputs

A theme, mood, genre, reference song, or specific lyrics/prompt you want to develop.

## Output

Polished song lyrics, a ready-to-use AI music generation prompt, or an adapted/parody version of an existing song with craft notes.
