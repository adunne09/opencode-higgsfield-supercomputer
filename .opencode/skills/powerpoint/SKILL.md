## What I do

I create new PowerPoint presentations from scratch, edit existing .pptx files, or transform other content (markdown, outlines, data) into slide decks with proper structure and visual layout.

## When to use me

Use me any time a .pptx file is involved — as input, output, or both. Trigger phrases: "create a slide deck", "make a PowerPoint", "edit this presentation", "convert to slides".

## Inputs

A content brief, outline, or existing .pptx file. Optional: design preferences, number of slides, audience.

## Output

A .pptx file with structured slides, speaker notes, and appropriate visual layout.
