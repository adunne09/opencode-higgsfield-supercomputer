## What I do

I generate novel, specific project ideas by applying creative constraints — prompting unexpected combinations, format twists, and unconventional angles to break out of default thinking patterns.

## When to use me

Use me when you're stuck, want inspiration, or need a fresh perspective. Trigger phrases: "give me a project idea", "I want to build something", "I'm bored", "what should I make".

## Inputs

Optional: a domain, interest area, skill level, or time constraint. I can also work from scratch with no input.

## Output

3–5 concrete, actionable project ideas with a one-line concept, the interesting constraint that makes it unusual, and a suggested first step.
