## What I do

I generate UGC-style tutorial videos where an AI creator demonstrates step-by-step use of a product, with on-screen Step N captions guiding the viewer through each stage.

## When to use me

Use me for how-to content, product walkthroughs, or educational demos. Requires: (a) tutorial/how-to/step-by-step intent, (b) UGC framing, (c) a specific product image or URL.

## Inputs

A product image or URL, optional step list or key demonstration points.

## Output

A UGC tutorial video with clear step sequencing and natural on-camera demonstration, formatted for short-form social.
