## What I do

I adapt a source video into a new version — swapping characters, replacing products, or preserving the original structure while changing key elements.

## When to use me

Use me when you have a reference video and want to recreate it with different subjects or brand elements. Trigger phrases: "adapt this video", "recreate this ad", "swap the character in this video", "replace the product".

## Inputs

A source video URL and a description of what to change (character swap, product replacement, or full recreation).

## Output

A new video that matches the source structure with the specified elements replaced. Note: this workflow is coming soon and may not be fully operational.
