## What I do

I generate UGC-style unboxing videos where an AI creator opens a package on camera, builds anticipation, and delivers a satisfying product reveal as the climax.

## When to use me

Use me when you need an unboxing, first-reaction, haul, or PR-drop video. Requires: (a) unboxing/reveal intent, (b) UGC framing, (c) a specific product image or URL.

## Inputs

A product image or URL, optional package reference photo, optional creator description.

## Output

A UGC unboxing video with authentic reveal energy, formatted for short-form social platforms.
