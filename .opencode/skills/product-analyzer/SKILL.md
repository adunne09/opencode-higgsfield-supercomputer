## What I do

I parse e-commerce product pages (Amazon, Shopify, AliExpress, and general product pages) and extract structured product data: name, description, key features, price, images, reviews summary, and brand.

## When to use me

Use me before any product media workflow when you need to ingest product information from a URL. Trigger phrases: "analyze product", "get product details from URL", "extract product info".

## Inputs

A publicly accessible product page URL.

## Output

Structured product data including name, brand, description, feature list, price, image URLs, and review highlights — ready to feed into image/video generation workflows.
