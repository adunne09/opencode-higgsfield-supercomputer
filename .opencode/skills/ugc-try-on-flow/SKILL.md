## What I do

I generate UGC-style try-on videos where an AI creator wears and poses with a wearable product, showcasing fit, texture, and style in a native social media format.

## When to use me

Use me for OOTD content, fit-check videos, or wearable product demos. Requires: (a) try-on/wearing/fit-check intent, (b) UGC framing, (c) a specific wearable product image or URL.

## Inputs

A wearable product image or URL, optional creator style description.

## Output

A UGC try-on video highlighting the wearable's real-world look and feel, formatted for short-form social.
