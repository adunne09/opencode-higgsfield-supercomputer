## What I do

I run organic marketing research: discover competitors, analyze current trends, identify winning content formats, and generate structured content briefs ready for production.

## When to use me

Use me at the start of an organic social media strategy or content calendar project. Trigger phrases: "organic marketing plan", "content strategy", "competitor analysis for social", "content calendar brief".

## Inputs

A brand name, product, or niche. Optional: target platforms, audience demographics, content goals.

## Output

A structured organic marketing research report with competitor profiles, trend analysis, winning format examples, and production-ready content briefs.
