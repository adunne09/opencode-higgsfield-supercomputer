## What I do

I handle all audio generation tasks: text-to-speech voiceover with custom voices, swapping the voice track in an existing video, and dubbing or translating a video into another language with lip-sync.

## When to use me

Use me for any audio-related task attached to video or standalone. Trigger phrases: "add voiceover", "change the voice in this video", "dub this to Spanish", "generate narration", "TTS".

## Inputs

For voiceover: a script and voice preference. For voice swap: a source video and target voice. For dubbing: a source video and target language.

## Output

An audio file with the generated voiceover, or a video with the new voice/dub track merged in.
