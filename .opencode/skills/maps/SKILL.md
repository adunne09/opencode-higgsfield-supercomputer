## What I do

I provide location intelligence: geocode a place name to coordinates, reverse-geocode coordinates to an address, find nearby places across 46 POI categories, and calculate driving/walking/cycling distances and travel times.

## When to use me

Use me for any location-based lookup or routing task. Trigger phrases: "find nearby", "how far is", "what's around", "geocode this address", "directions from X to Y".

## Inputs

A place name, address, coordinates, or two locations for routing.

## Output

Geocoded coordinates, a list of nearby POIs with distances, or a route with distance and estimated travel time.
