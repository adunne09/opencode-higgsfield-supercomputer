## What I do

I generate high-grade 15-second 16:9 format video ads and commercials for products, brands, or services — with broadcast-quality production values, scripted beat structure, and strong brand presence.

## When to use me

Use me when you need a premium video ad or commercial. Trigger phrases: "TV ad", "TV commercial", "create an ad", "make a commercial", "product ad", "premium video ad", "lifestyle ad".

## Inputs

A product image or URL, brand name, optional tagline or messaging direction.

## Output

A 15-second 16:9 video ad suitable for television, YouTube pre-roll, and connected TV platforms.
