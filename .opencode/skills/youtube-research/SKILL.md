## What I do

I research YouTube data: search for videos by topic, look up channel information and subscriber counts, fetch video metadata (views, likes, publish date), and read top comments.

## When to use me

Use me for YouTube competitor research, trend discovery, or audience analysis. Trigger phrases: "research YouTube channels", "find videos about X", "how many subscribers", "what are people saying in comments".

## Inputs

A search query, channel name/URL, or video URL.

## Output

Structured YouTube research data: channel stats, video metadata, search results ranked by relevance, or comment sentiment summary.
