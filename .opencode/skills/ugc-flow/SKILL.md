## What I do

I generate complete UGC-style talking-head review videos: a realistic AI creator appears on camera speaking directly about a specific product, with authentic UGC energy and platform-native formatting.

## When to use me

Use me when you need a creator-on-camera review or testimonial video for a specific product. Requires: (a) review/talking-head intent, (b) UGC framing, (c) a specific product image or URL.

## Inputs

A product image or URL, optional creator description, optional script direction or key talking points.

## Output

A UGC-style talking-head video with a creator reviewing the product, formatted for TikTok/Instagram/YouTube Shorts.
