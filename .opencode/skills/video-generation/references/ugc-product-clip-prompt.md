# UGC Product Clip Prompt Writer -- Seedance 2.0

Product-only UGC clip (voiceover only, no on-camera creator speaking).
Board: 21:9 strip, FOUR vertical 9:16 slots -> FOUR hard cuts.
Arc (Board 1): PRODUCT-INTRO -> PRODUCT-DEMO-A -> PRODUCT-DEMO-B -> PRODUCT-RESULT.

## Key differences from ugc-clip-prompt.md
- Voice-over only (off-screen): character mouth CLOSED in every Cut, no lip-sync
- 4 cuts not 3
- Product is the HERO of every cut (auxiliary person is supporting cast, cropped/partial only)
- iPhone UGC aesthetic enforcement mandatory (NOT editorial product photography)

## Prompt structure

```
Style & Mood: UGC iPhone aesthetic -- phone-sensor grain, natural ambient light, NOT studio strobes, NOT editorial product photography; [TRIPOD/FIRST-PERSON-POV/MIXED POV cadence], social media vertical format.

Narrative Summary: [1 sentence, product-demo story, 4-cut throughline].

Dynamic Description:
Cut 1 (0-Xs) -- [framing] [POV]: [PRODUCT-INTRO -- product in native context, no active demo yet]. Hard cut to.
Cut 2 (Xs-Ys) -- [framing] [POV]: [PRODUCT-DEMO-A -- first demo angle, mechanism actuates]. Hard cut to.
Cut 3 (Ys-Zs) -- [framing] [POV]: [PRODUCT-DEMO-B -- distinctly different second demo angle]. Hard cut to.
Cut 4 (Zs-end) -- [framing] [POV]: [PRODUCT-RESULT -- result state or final hero shot].

Static Description: [1-2 sentences: setting].

Audio: Off-screen voiceover, [she/he per voice_gender] describes product benefits, emotional UGC tone, NOT on-camera dialogue, NO lip-sync: "[voiceover_segment distributed across 4 cuts]"

Facial features clear, mouth closed throughout. Shot on iPhone, casual handheld framing, natural ambient light, phone-sensor grain, no retouch, no professional gloss. [POV movement language]. No on-screen text, no subtitles, no captions, no watermarks.
```

## iPhone aesthetic enforcement (mandatory)
Include in every Cut AND Style & Mood / Quality Suffix:
- "Shot on iPhone, casual handheld framing"
- "Phone-sensor grain and realistic surface texture preserved -- no retouch, no professional gloss"
- "Authentic UGC creator phone capture, NOT editorial product photography, NOT studio shoot, NOT magazine commercial"

HARD BAN: dramatic side-lighting, cinematic lighting, editorial product photography, shallow depth of field, softbox, ring light, magazine retouch, golden hour

## Cut 2 vs Cut 3 must be visually DIFFERENT (different action/angle/target)
Cut 4 MUST be visually distinct from Cut 1 (different angle/scale/composition)

## Voiceover rules
- No greetings, no product re-introductions (even Board 1)
- Opens mid-thought with benefit/capability/sensory descriptor
- No AI-tell phrases (obsessed, you have to try this, game changer, 10/10)

## Closing quality suffix block (mandatory)
"Authentic UGC creator phone capture -- NOT editorial product photography, NOT studio shoot, NOT magazine commercial. Phone-sensor grain and realistic textures preserved, no retouch, no professional gloss."
