# UGC Unboxing Clip Prompt Writer -- Seedance 2.0

UGC unboxing: creator opens a delivery package, product reveal is the climax.
Board: 21:9 strip, FOUR vertical 9:16 slots -> FOUR hard cuts.
Board 1 canonical arc: PACKED -> REVEAL -> PRODUCT-FOCUS -> SATISFACTION.

## Box state per cut (mandatory)
- Cut 1 PACKED: sealed box is focal object. Product NOT visible. Character interacts with box.
  At end of Cut 1: character picks up a small utility knife, slices packing tape (one quick action),
  sets knife aside. Box flaps fall open, color-matched packing/tissue paper briefly visible.
- Cut 2 REVEAL: product just emerging from/nestled in color-matched tissue paper inside open box.
  Box may be at frame edge (fading from focus). Do NOT describe opening motion here.
- Cut 3 PRODUCT-FOCUS: box GONE. Product is hero -- extended toward lens, on palms, held up.
- Cut 4 SATISFACTION: box GONE. Character + product only, settled confident pose.

Once box disappears: NEVER re-introduce it.

## Prompt structure

```
Style & Mood: UGC iPhone aesthetic, [light], [MIXED/TRIPOD/SELFIE POV cadence], social media vertical format.

Narrative Summary: [1 sentence, arc_role throughline of 4 cuts].

Dynamic Description:
Cut 1 (0-Xs) -- [framing] [POV]: [PACKED -- box interaction, knife slice, box opens]. Hard cut to.
Cut 2 (Xs-Ys) -- [framing] [POV]: [REVEAL -- product emerges, peak reaction]. Hard cut to.
Cut 3 (Ys-Zs) -- [framing] [POV]: [PRODUCT-FOCUS -- box gone, product as hero]. Hard cut to.
Cut 4 (Zs-end) -- [framing] [POV]: [SATISFACTION -- box gone, settled with product].

Static Description: [1-2 sentences].

Audio: She speaks to camera, iPhone microphone audio with natural room tone[, K=1 non-verbal sounds]: "[monologue distributed across 4 cuts]"

Facial features clear, consistent clothing. Shot on iPhone, natural lighting. [POV movement language]. No on-screen text, no subtitles, no captions, no watermarks.
```

## Cut timing
| duration | Cut 1 | Cut 2 | Cut 3 | Cut 4 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 10s | 2.5s | 3s | 2s | 2.5s |
| 15s | 3.5s | 4.5s | 3s | 4s |

## Canonical Board 1 POV cadence
TRIPOD -> TRIPOD -> TRIPOD-CLOSE -> SELFIE
(PACKED/REVEAL in tripod for two-handed package handling; SATISFACTION in selfie)

## Weight & Grip logic
Heavy: 2 hands, visible strain. Bulky-light: 2 hands, no strain. Light: 1 hand. Tiny: pinched.
