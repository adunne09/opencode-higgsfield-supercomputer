## What I do

I research social media trends, analyze ad intelligence data, and discover winning content formats across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, YouTube Shorts, and Meta — surfacing what is performing right now.

## When to use me

Use me for competitive research, content strategy, or before any creative production workflow. Trigger phrases: "what's trending", "research competitors", "find winning content formats", "ad intelligence", "what ads are running".

## Inputs

A brand, product category, competitor name, or content niche. Optional: specific platform focus.

## Output

A trend report with top-performing content formats, winning creative patterns, competitor analysis, and recommendations for content that is likely to perform.
