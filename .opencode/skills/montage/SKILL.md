## What I do

I assemble multiple video clips into a single file, overlay background music, attach or burn-in subtitles, and apply basic post-processing like transitions and trim points.

## When to use me

Use me when you have existing video files that need to be combined or finished. Trigger phrases: "stitch these clips", "add music to video", "combine videos", "burn subtitles", "make a montage".

## Inputs

Video file paths or URLs, optional audio track, optional subtitle file, and assembly instructions (order, trim points, transition style).

## Output

A single finished video file with all clips assembled, music mixed, and subtitles applied.
