## What I do

I provide access to 54 production-quality design systems extracted from real websites like Stripe, Linear, Vercel, Notion, and others — letting you match the visual identity of industry-leading products.

## When to use me

Use me when building a website or UI and you want to reference or replicate the visual style of a specific well-known product. Trigger phrases: "make it look like Stripe", "use a Linear-style design", "Vercel aesthetic", "copy this site's style".

## Inputs

The name of the target website or design style you want to match.

## Output

Design tokens, color palettes, typography choices, component patterns, and layout guidelines extracted from the target site's production design system.
