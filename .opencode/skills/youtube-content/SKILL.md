## What I do

I fetch transcripts from YouTube videos and transform them into structured text content: chapter breakdowns, concise summaries, Twitter/X threads, or long-form blog posts.

## When to use me

Use me when you want to repurpose YouTube video content into written form. Trigger phrases: "summarize this YouTube video", "turn this video into a blog post", "get transcript", "make a thread from this video".

## Inputs

A YouTube video URL.

## Output

The requested text format: chapter list with timestamps, executive summary, tweet thread, or a full blog post derived from the video transcript.
