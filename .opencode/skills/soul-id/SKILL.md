## What I do

I train and manage persistent face identity models (Soul IDs) from 1–100 portrait photos, or bootstrap one from a text description. Once trained, a Soul ID can be used across any generation to maintain consistent facial identity.

## When to use me

Use me when you need a reusable face identity for repeated use across images and videos. Trigger phrases: "train a face model", "create a Soul ID", "make a digital twin", "reusable character face".

## Inputs

1–100 portrait photos of the subject, OR a detailed text description of the face. Optional: a display name.

## Output

A trained Soul ID (UUID) that can be referenced in any future image or video generation to maintain consistent facial identity.
