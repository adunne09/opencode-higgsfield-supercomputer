## What I do

I analyze a brand's website and extract its core visual and messaging assets: logo, primary and secondary color palette (hex codes), typography choices, hero imagery style, tagline, and target audience signals.

## When to use me

Use me at the start of any brand or marketing project when you need a structured brand profile. Trigger phrases: "extract brand assets", "analyze brand", "get brand colors from URL", "brand profile".

## Inputs

A publicly accessible website URL for the brand you want to analyze.

## Output

A structured brand asset report with logo description, hex color palette, font names, imagery style, tagline, and inferred target audience.
